Welcome to Knit

This installer will place Knit in the following locations:

  • /Applications/Knit.app — registers the icon for .knit files
  • /usr/local/bin/knit — command-line tool
  • /Library/Services/Knit *.workflow — Finder Quick Actions (right-click menu)

After installation, right-click any file or folder in Finder to find these new items:

  • Knit Compress (ZIP) — produces a standard ZIP archive (extractable anywhere)
  • Knit Compress (.knit) — produces Knit's high-speed .knit archive
  • Knit Extract — extracts ZIP or .knit archives

Requirements: macOS 15 Sequoia or later, Apple Silicon (M1–M5) Mac.
You will be asked for your administrator password.
